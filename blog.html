Natural Environment

The five elements of yoga like air,water,fire,earth and space are connected to the environment . The imbalance in any of them will cause problems for mankind . We need to preserve our earth by leading a sustainable lifestyle where the importance of yoga is known to everyone . Yoga in nature is pretty much exactly what it sounds like-it's taking your yoga practice out of the sweaty studio and into nature .

Friendly Environment

The eco-friendly practices that are supposed to reduce pollution and support nature are referred to as environment -friendly practice . Reduce ,reuse and recycle are the three resources that are meant to support nature . Plastic bags are non biodegradable and cause soil erosin and water pollution . It means reduces the burden on the environment and harm to its inhabitants .
